To install on iPhone/iPad:

  1. Tap the ⬆️ Share button (bottom)
  2. Find "Add to Home Screen"
  3. Tap "Add"

The app installs as an icon on your screen.

To install on Android:

  1. Tap the menu ⋮ (three dots top-right)
  2. Select "Install app" or "Add to Home Screen"
  3. Confirm "Install"

✅ App installed!
